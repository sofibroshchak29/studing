The main trends of the fall-winter 2020 season

It is quite obvious that designers strive for a universal wardrobe, out of seasons and clear trends,
hence floor-length sheath dresses for the evening, strict coats and suits always and everywhere.
On the other hand, the severity was opposed by the craving for home comfort, which found expression
in patchwork, quilted "blankets", oversized cardigans and carpet prints.

The fall-winter 2020 shows were not without theatricality and trends of past eras: there are silhouettes
of Marie Antoinette's dresses, embroidered frock coats, and a costume chorus at the Louis Vuitton show.
Plus endless references to religious themes - monastic silhouettes, crosses, covered heads and closed faces.

Demna Gvasalia showed his version of the fashion apocalypse at all, sinking the first rows of guest chairs under
the water, along which the models walked to the accompaniment of the end of the world broadcast on the LED panel,
which made his show for Balenciaga the most spectacular and memorable show of the season.

    The main trends this fall and winter include:

  • Bulky shoulders and sleeves
  • Leather clothes
  • Total black
  • Pantsuits
  • Sequins
  • Cell
  • Patchwork

And now you will read more detailed information about the sensational trends.

Closed face

If you prefer to go out to people incognito, this trend is definitely for you. Hand in hand with the fashion
for the most closed modest dresses is the desire of designers to hide the faces of models behind masks.

Modest black dresses to the floor

Another very important theme of the entire Fashion Week season is genuine modesty. The sexy miniature dresses
were replaced by the most restrained, puritanical, with long sleeves and floor-length skirts.
The most spectacular, in our opinion, are completely black.

Dramatic embroidered frock coats

Theatricality at Fashion Weeks this time was especially in high esteem. Confirmation is the love of designers
for dramatic embroidered jackets, which are more reminiscent of stage costumes for Shakespeare's tragedies.

Mens gray coats

As you know, without classics anywhere. One of the main things next winter is a strict man's gray coat, which
will definitely become your best friend for many seasons. Look for the most correct and beautiful versions
in the collections of Alberta Ferretti, Sportmax, Prada and many other brands.

Exquisite latex

Latex in the next fall-winter season will open up a new side for fashionistas. Instead of dresses with
a touch of BDSM aesthetics, we choose suits, long skirts and dresses with draperies from iridescent fabric.

Strict two with a skirt

The most desirable and fashionable ensemble of the next season is a strict wool jacket with a leather belt
and a skirt below the knee with a high side slit. It is your killer tool for your most important business
negotiations. These suits can be worn both with shoes and with high boots.

Colorful carpets

The most fashionable in six months will dress in real carpets. We remember the masterpieces that adorned
the walls of our grandparents' apartments, and we go shopping with just such references.

Fluffy princess dresses

If you are a fan of the Crown TV series, then you will definitely appreciate this trend. Instead of
the usual evening gowns, we choose outfits that are worthy of the ballrooms of kings and queens. L
ush skirts, satin and silk ribbons, embroidery and pearls - we learn to dress in the spirit of the aristocracy.

Extremely long fringe

Fringe is one of the most important trends for the coming season. Moreover, the designers urge you to be guided
by the "the longer the better" attitude and choose dresses, skirts, jackets and even bags with the longest fringe
made of leather and fabric. She will make each of your bows airy and agile.

Retro style double-breasted coats

Designers urge to abandon experiments with outerwear in the coming season and look for inspiration in the past.
So, almost the most cited in the collections was the classic double-breasted coat with large gold buttons,
this is what we saw at the shows of Loewe, Givenchy, JW Anderson and many other brands.

Fashionable windbreakers

This functional wardrobe attribute of hikers and small children promises to become the most fashionable in six months.
Moreover, Nicolas Ghesquière, Virgil Abloh and Donatella Versace suggest combining windbreakers with girlish dresses,
quilted skirts and transparent organza trousers to create a strong contrast.

Intricate art prints

Clothes should be a real work of art. This slogan was used by designers at Fashion Weeks. The most complex art prints
created by Julio Pomar and Gregory Crewdson, respectively, appeared in the Kenzo and Namacheko collections,
and Natasha Ramsay-Levy at Chloé invited the artist Rita Ackermann to work on the fall-winter 2020 collection.

Fisherman Hats

Fisherman's hats did not leave indifferent the designers this February and March. Several brands have developed
their own variations at once. Our favorites are Christophe Lehmer, Felipe Oliveira Baptista and Samuel Drira.

However, remember, you do not need to follow trends. Everyone is a separate person,
so the main thing is that you were comfortable and you felt confident and unsurpassed!
